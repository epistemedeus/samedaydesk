What separated the A's from the D's

The top scorers weren't doing anything exotic. They simply got all of the fundamentals right. The laggards typically missed two of them:

  • JSON-LD structured data. The single biggest differentiator. A-graders ship Organization (and often Service/FAQ) schema so AI engines can classify and quote them; the D-graders shipped little or none.
  • Complete Open Graph & meta tags. Clean title, description, and OG tags so engines and social previews render them correctly.
  • AI-crawler access. A welcoming robots.txt that doesn't block GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, or Google-Extended.
  • An XML sitemap linked from robots.txt.

The irony writes itself: the two firms that scored a D both sell search visibility. If you run an agency, the AI-readiness of your own site (and every client site you manage) is now part of the product.
