We scanned 19 top DTC brands for AI-search readiness. Warby Parker scored a D.

Shoppers are starting to ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI what to buy. So we ran 19 of the best-known direct-to-consumer brands through an AI-search readiness check. Most do well, but several big names are leaving themselves hard for AI shopping assistants to read and recommend.

What we found

  • The leaders nail it. Casper, Brooklinen, Magic Spoon, Purple, and Mejuri all scored 100, with proper structured data, crawlable robots, and clean metadata.
  • Structured data is the gap. 7 of 19 brands (37%) ship no JSON-LD on the homepage, including Warby Parker, Glossier, Bombas, Ridge, Hydro Flask, Allbirds, and Liquid Death. Product and FAQ schema are exactly what AI shopping assistants lean on.
  • Warby Parker is the standout miss at 45/100: no structured data and no Open Graph tags on the homepage, despite being one of the most recognized DTC brands.
